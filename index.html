About

A flexible remote teammate for modern teams

I started out in remote technical and customer support — handling high-volume chat and email queues, troubleshooting account and billing issues, and documenting everything carefully in CRM systems. That background trained me to be organized, responsive, and easy to manage across time zones.

More recently I've expanded into AI-assisted content work: researching a brand's audience and trends, then drafting and managing SEO blog articles using Claude and ChatGPT to speed up the process without losing the client's voice. I pair that with design and video — using Photoshop, Canva, Adobe Premiere Pro and CapCut (also AI-enhanced) to turn that content into graphics and short-form video.

In short: if your team needs someone who can answer support tickets in the morning, draft a blog post in the afternoon, and cut a quick promo video for it — that's the kind of work I enjoy.
